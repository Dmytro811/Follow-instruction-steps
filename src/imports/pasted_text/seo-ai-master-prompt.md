PRODUCT OVERVIEW
The product name is:
SEO AI
This is NOT a traditional SEO agency.
This is an AI Visibility Platform.
Its mission is helping businesses optimize their websites for AI assistants and Large Language Models (LLMs).
Traditional SEO focuses on Google rankings.
SEO AI focuses on helping websites become understandable, trustworthy and recommendable by AI systems.
The platform analyzes whether a website is optimized for AI recommendation engines and provides actionable improvements.
